HOW TO PLAY
Move your mouse over a snail to catch it. If any part of your crosshair touches a snail, it counts.
Each wave adds more snails. Catch them all to clear the wave.
Watch out! Snails can hit you with football and basketball moves (jukes, trucks, crossovers, etc.) that freeze your cursor and let them escape.
Rarity matters. Snails range from Common (easy) to Celestial (nearly impossible). Higher rarity = faster, smaller hitbox, trickier behavior.
Difficulty changes randomly each wave. Higher difficulty = faster snails, more football moves, rarer spawns.
Earn awards by catching snails, surviving moves, clearing waves, and more. Check the AWARDS tab anytime.
